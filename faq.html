What are collaborations ( collab ) ? Collaborations in web 3 specifically in NFTs means Collab between two NFT Communities. It is the project which is collabing with an already existing collection/alpha group. To make it even simpler. Collabs = Web 3 way of Marketing As when you are collabing with an existing NFT community they are promoting our collection to their Holders/community. ( This is not paid promo as we aren’t paying them money.) They are given Whitelist/allowlist spots in exchange for promoting us. Summary - Collaboration in web 3 means Giving of whitelist/allowlist spots in exchange of promotion in discord/twitter. What are whitelist/allowlist spots? Allowlist spots means giving early access to the minting phase at a cheaper price than the public sale. ( Same like a STOCK IPO ) Example Allowlist sale = 18th November at 0.1 eth Public Sale = 19th November at 0.15 eth How are collabs done? 1. They are inform on Giveaways in discord and twitter 2. The giveaways are hosted using Alphabot/Premint ( Whitelist Tools ) 3. The winners needs to follow discord or follow twitter ( or other requirements ) to enter the giveaway 4. After 24hrs the winners are chosen. 5. The wallets of the winners are then collected and given to us and then these wallets are added to the contract and these people are whitelisted! This will give you an idea of what the collaboration process looks like so you have an idea of what you should be expecting. Alphabot Link - https://www.alphabot.app/lookswhale-x-project-godjira-frk1ri ( This is one got 300 entries, not seen on the website, but I have the info ) Premint link - https://www.premint.xyz/tentacular-1/ ( this one got 60 entries ) Both are WL Tools and are used to do Collaborations. Collaborations solve it and give edge to any web 2 brand/IP entering into web 3. Why Many Projects didn’t do Collabs early 2022??
